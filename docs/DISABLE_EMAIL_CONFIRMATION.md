# How to Disable Email Confirmation in Supabase

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Open Supabase Dashboard
1. Go to: https://supabase.com/dashboard
2. Select your project: `njwlzneexobgyypuijnt`

### Step 2: Go to Authentication Settings
1. In the left sidebar, click **"Authentication"**
2. Click **"Settings"** (or look for "Email Auth" section)

### Step 3: Disable Email Confirmation
1. Scroll down to find **"Email Auth"** section
2. Look for **"Enable email confirmations"** toggle
3. **Turn it OFF** (disable it)
4. Click **"Save"** at the bottom

### Step 4: Test Registration
1. Go back to your app
2. Try registering a new account
3. You should be able to sign in immediately without email confirmation

---

**Note:** This is recommended for development. For production, you may want to keep email confirmation enabled for security.
